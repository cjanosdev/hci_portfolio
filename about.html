About Me

Hi, I’m Catrina Janos. In this HCI course, I learned about user research, usability, accessibility, prototyping, evaluation, reporting, and interaction design. This portfolio displays my main course projects and explains what I worked on.

I am interested in design that feels usable, accessible, minimal yet still aesthetically pleasing, clear, helpful, and easy to use. I tried to connect each assignment to the HCI theory we were learning in class and to think about how users would feel while using any of the user interfaces we redesigned or analyzed in this course.

What is in this portfolio

The Projects page has short descriptions and images for my course work. The Docs page links to the full design writeups. The Links page includes my Notion and Medium work.
